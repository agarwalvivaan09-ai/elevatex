Social Welfare Initiatives

ElevateX conducts structured social welfare drives focused on addressing immediate community needs while creating long-term impact. These initiatives range from environmental action to direct support for underserved communities, executed through a consistent, scalable model led by students.

Drive Planning Department

All welfare initiatives are managed by the Drive Planning Department, responsible for ideation, logistics, on-ground execution, and coordination. This ensures that each drive is not a one-off activity, but part of a structured and repeatable system.
