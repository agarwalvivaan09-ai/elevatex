Founder’s Note

Hi, I’m Samridh, one of the founding members of ELEVATE-X.

The story of ELEVATE-X begins on the night of 16th December 2024, when Vivaan called me and proposed the idea of starting a nonprofit organisation. The concept was still in its early stages, yet it was driven by a clear sense of purpose and passion.

In the midst of our pre-board examinations, Vivaan, Devyansh, and I began working on ELEVATE-X, dedicating countless late nights to turning our vision into reality. On 8th March, ELEVATE-X officially came into existence.

We founded ELEVATE-X as a teen-led nonprofit with the objective of providing young minds with hands-on experience in areas such as finance, execution, and operations, while simultaneously contributing to the betterment of society.

The organisation was designed to bridge the gap between academic learning and real-world impact, empowering students to apply their skills in meaningful ways.

In my role as CFO, I gained valuable experience in financial management, risk mitigation, and strategic planning. I was responsible for overseeing fundraising initiatives, managing organisational funds, and ensuring that each campaign remained financially sustainable.

This experience not only strengthened my technical skills but also deepened my sense of accountability and responsibility toward the organisation’s mission.

ELEVATE-X, to me, represents a community of like-minded individuals who are committed to creating positive change in both their own lives and in society.

If you possess the drive and dedication to contribute to a better future, ELEVATE-X offers a structured, purpose-driven platform to begin your journey.

— Samridh Sanganeria
Founder, ElevateX
