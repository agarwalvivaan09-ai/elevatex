What is Project Unnati?

Project Unnati is ElevateX’s financial literacy initiative focused on delivering practical, real-world education to underserved communities. Through structured workshops, the program introduces students to essential financial concepts such as saving, budgeting, and basic investing — areas that are often overlooked in traditional education.

ElevateX collaborates with local NGOs and community groups to reach students who lack access to financial awareness, ensuring that knowledge is not just theoretical but directly applicable to everyday life.
