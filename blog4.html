From Member to Leadership: My Journey at ElevateX

I joined ElevateX last year as a general member, beginning my journey in the Drive Execution Team. During this time, I actively participated in multiple drives and contributed consistently to their smooth and efficient execution.

Beyond my assigned responsibilities, I took initiative to deepen my involvement by understanding how operations functioned at a broader level. One of the most defining experiences during this phase was independently hosting a drive. Taking full ownership of an initiative strengthened my leadership, planning, and organizational abilities while pushing me beyond my comfort zone.

Recognizing my dedication and performance, I was promoted to an Executive Member (X Member), and subsequently took on the role of Head of the Drive Execution Team. In this capacity, my responsibilities expanded from execution to leadership. I was involved in planning initiatives, coordinating with team members, and ensuring that each project was delivered efficiently and to a high standard.

This year, I also played a key role in organizing our flagship event, the Winterland Carnival, “Euphoria.” As a core committee member, I was responsible for securing sponsors and onboarding multiple stalls, both of which were critical to the scale and success of the event.

In addition, I served as the Head of Security for the event. This role required managing safety protocols, coordinating security arrangements, and ensuring a smooth and secure experience for all attendees. It demanded attention to detail, quick decision-making, and strong coordination throughout the event.

My journey at ElevateX has been incredibly enriching. It has enabled me to develop essential skills in leadership, teamwork, communication, and event management, while also building the confidence to take initiative and handle responsibilities at a much larger scale.

ElevateX has provided me with an environment where growth is not just encouraged, but expected — an environment where individuals are trusted with responsibility and given the opportunity to learn through real experience.
