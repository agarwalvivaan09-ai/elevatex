Fundraising at ElevateX

Fundraising at ElevateX is not limited to isolated events — it is a coordinated, organization-wide effort. Each initiative brings together multiple departments, combining planning, execution, and outreach to create high-impact experiences while generating resources for future initiatives.

How It Works

The Project Operations team leads planning and execution, while the Finance Department oversees budgeting and fund management. Marketing and Outreach drives visibility through social media and partnerships. Fundraising is one of the few verticals where the entire organization collaborates — making it a true reflection of ElevateX’s structure and scale.

Our Fundraisers

Tech Tonik • 10 May 2025

Tech Tonik marked ElevateX’s first-ever fundraising event — a neon-themed techno night that brought together students for an energetic and immersive experience. With over 100 attendees, the event laid the foundation for future initiatives. While the team faced early-stage challenges, Tech Tonik became a defining learning experience that shaped how ElevateX approached scale and execution.

100+ Attendees

Retrograde • 20 July 2025

Building on the lessons from Tech Tonik, Retrograde introduced a retro red-carpet prom theme, creating a more refined and immersive experience. With over 160 attendees, the event saw significant growth in scale and execution. The evening combined aesthetics, energy, and engagement — making it one of the most memorable nights for the team and participants alike.

160+ Attendees • 160 Saplings Planted

Zenith • 17 August 2025

Zenith marked ElevateX’s expansion into structured, idea-driven competitions. Designed as an online Shark Tank-style event, it provided a platform for students to pitch innovative ideas. With 14 teams participating and free entry, Zenith emphasized accessibility while maintaining high engagement.

14 Teams Participated

Football Fiesta • 9 October 2025

Football Fiesta marked ElevateX’s entry into the sports domain and its first step toward external sponsorships. The event brought together 14 teams, combining competition with large-scale coordination. It served as a key learning milestone in managing logistics, partnerships, and event execution at scale.

14 Teams • First Sponsored Event

Euphoria: Winterland • 13 December 2025

Euphoria stands as ElevateX’s largest and most ambitious fundraising event to date. Designed as a large-scale winter-themed experience, it brought together over 450 attendees. The event represented the culmination of months of planning, collaboration, and execution — showcasing the full potential of a structured student-led organization.

450+ Attendees
