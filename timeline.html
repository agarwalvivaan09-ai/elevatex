The Journey of ElevateX

From a single idea to a growing student-led movement.

8 Mar 2025

The Idea

ElevateX was first conceptualized as a way to support cancer patients later evolving into Project Unnati and other welfare schemes.

26 Apr 2025

First Drive

The first initiative was executed, marking the transition from idea to action. It was a cleanup drive at Vectoria Memorial, Kolkata.

6 May 2025

100 Members

The community rapidly grew, reaching its first major milestone of 100 members.

10 May 2025

Tech Tonik

The first fundraiser — a bold first step into large-scale execution and event management.

20 Jun 2025

Project Unnati Launched

Financial literacy initiative introduced, expanding ElevateX into structured social impact.

20 Jul 2025

Retrograde

A major leap in execution — larger scale, stronger systems, and refined coordination.

17 Aug 2025

Zenith

First online Shark Tank-style event with student founders pitching ideas.

2 Sept 2025

200 Members

Rapid expansion continued as the community doubled in size.

8 Oct 2025

Football Fiesta

First sports event — expanding into new domains and introducing sponsorships.

17 Nov 2025

300 Members

Continued growth reinforced ElevateX as a structured and scalable student platform.

13 Dec 2025

Euphoria

Flagship fundraiser with 450+ attendees — the largest and most ambitious event yet.

15 Jan 2026

National Collaboration

ElevateX expanded beyond local operations, entering the national stage.
